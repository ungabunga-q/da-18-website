Death Case Procedures

The complete procedural guide for handling share transfers after a member's death.

Key Concepts

Provisional vs. Primary Membership

Provisional: A temporary "trustee" status for nominees/heirs. Can occupy the flat but cannot sell or rent it.

Primary: Full "ownership" status, allowing sale/rent. Requires court documents (e.g., Succession Certificate).

Nominee vs. Legal Heir

Nominee: A person appointed by the member. Acts as a custodian or trustee for the legal heirs.

Legal Heir: The person(s) legally entitled to the property by law. Only a court can determine legal heirship.

The "Provisional Member" Stamp

This is the most critical step in both scenarios. The new member's name MUST be endorsed on the share certificate and all society records with the words "Provisional Member".
